It is well recognized that major entertainment projects are the primary goal upon which the vision of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia 2030 is built, an initiative led by His Royal Highness Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman. This is part of the vision's programs aiming for achievement. Among these programs are the development of the financial, health, housing, industrial, and entertainment sectors. Notable projects under this vision include 1- Qiddiya Project, 2- Red Sea Project, 3- NEOM Project, 4- Roshn Project, 5- Diriyah Project, all of which incorporate significant entertainment and tourism projects. Our company, Sabmerina, serves as the exclusive agent for several foreign companies, including Solkaf from Austria, which specializes in building ice hotels and indoor ski halls; Subibor from Spain, known for manufacturing yachts and tourist submarines; Safari Zoo from Malaysia; Parque Madrid from Spain, specializing in building safari zoos; Blue Ice from Germany, which focuses on constructing ice villages, toboggans, and ski slopes; Wimpleg from France, known for building marine cable cars and bridges; and Aqua Florida from the USA, specialized in building marine life museums and aquariums. We are glad to tell you that Sabmerina has thirty years of experience in major distinctive entertainment and tourism projects, in addition to its clients from the aforementioned foreign companies. These projects, which align with the goals of Vision 2030 and have been approved by the Ministry of Tourism and the Public Investment Fund, include: 1- The world's first permanent ice hotel and the world's first semi-ice hotel along with mountain ski halls and ice cafes, 2- The largest safari zoo proposed to be built in Taif, the first in the Kingdom, 3- Tourist submarines and semi-submarine projects and skydiving club, 4- The cable car projects for Jabal An-Nur and Jabal Ath-Thawr, submitted to the Makkah Region Development Authority awaiting approval, 5- Water parks and entertainment cities, 6- The world's first Jeddah Corniche cable car project, and the Riyadh Eye and Jeddah Eye, the first in the Kingdom..
